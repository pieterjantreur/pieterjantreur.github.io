1. How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?
Answ: Connect to the printer and print your document.
2. How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?
Answ: Go to a printer, scan your HZ-pass and scan the document. the scan will automatically be sent to your e-mail.
3. How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event) on the HZ web shop?
Answ: Sign in to your HZ account. Either go to webshop.hz.nl or go to the webshop by using the hz portal. once on the webshop choose your item of choice. fill everything in as required and go to checkout where youll have to choose your payment method and check out.
4. How can you book a project space in one of the wings?
Answ: From the HZ portal go to the Selfservice portal, go to "Make a reservation", go to "Reserve meeting room or project group room", go to "reserve a room", there youll see every available moment to reserve a room.
5. What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?
Answ: Park it in the parking lot of the AH.
